Hypers Dynasty Fantasy Football League Rules

1. League Overview

Welcome to the Hypers Dynasty Fantasy Football League. This is a long-term, competitive league where teams retain their rosters from season to season. Owners must be committed to active participation year-round.

2. League Format

Teams: 12 teams

Roster Size: 25 players per team

Starting Lineup: QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, TE, 2 RB/WR/TE

Scoring: Standard scoring, 5 points per passing touchdown. [TODO]

Playoffs: 6 Team playoff starting in week 15

3. Rookie Draft

Rookie Draft: Annual draft for incoming NFL rookies held [DATE][TODO]

Draft Order: Picks 1 thru 6 are determined by max potential points for (Max PPF) of the 6 teams that did not make playoffs. Picks 7 and 8 are determined by max PPF of the 5th and 6th place finish. Picks 9 thru 12 are determined by final standings of 1st thru 4th place.

4. Roster Management

Waivers: FAAB

Trades: Trades allowed from restart of league year until trade deadline in week 11. Trade deadline set to disable trades at the beggining of the first game of the week.

Injured Reserve (IR): 2 IR spots for IR designated players ONLY

Taxi Squad: No taxi squad

5. Season Play & Schedule

Divisions: 3 4-tream divisons

Regular Season: 14 week regular season. Each team plays each other team once in the season, and in division teams an additional time.

Playoffs Format: 6 Team playoff starting in week 15. Single round single elimination.

Tie-breakers: Don't tie

6. Fees & Payouts

Entry Fee: $25

Payout Structure: 1st - $175

2nd - $100

3rd - $25

7. Rule Changes & Governance

Voting Process: Rule changes require majority vote (7/12) for experemental changes, and supermajority (10/12) for permenant rules changes. Any rules changes that change the fundemental structure of the league(e.g. superflex), or effects payout structure or buy ins require unanimous agreement.

Commissioner Role: The commissioner has the final say on rule interpretation and league disputes

8. Code of Conduct

All members are expected to participate actively, communicate respectfully, and adhere to fair play principles. Collusion, tanking, and inactivity may result in penalties or removal from the league.
